How it works

Simple enough to trust. Structured enough to scale.

Build your structure once, record activity as it happens, and let the dashboard stay organized.

Create portfolios

Organize investments by strategy, asset type, goal, or any structure that matches how you think.

02

Features

Everything important, without the clutter.

A focused toolkit for investors who want reliable records and readable performance.

Multi-portfolio tracking

Separate long-term holdings, experiments, income strategies, or custom goals without mixing the records.

Transaction history

Track buys, sells, deposits, withdrawals, dividends, fees, dates, and notes with clear editability.

Performance math

Review average cost, realized gains and losses, ROI, dividends, and cash position from your entries.

Visual summaries

Use allocation charts and symbol-level heatmaps to understand where results are coming from.
